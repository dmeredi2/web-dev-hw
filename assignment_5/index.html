Cool Kids Kool-Aid

Ingredients

  • 1 package of Black Cherry Kool-Aid
  • 1/2 gallon of water
  • 4 cups of ice
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 shot of vodka per serving

Directions

  1. Heat up 2 cups of water until boiling
  2. In a large pitcher add hot water, sugar, and Kool-Aid mix
  3. Stir mixture until disolved
  4. Add 4 cups of ice to mixture to cool down the water
  5. Fill the remainding amount of the pitcher with water to the 1/2 gallon mark, and stir vigorusly
  6. Pour 1 glass of Kool-Aid mixture over ice and 1 shot of vodka
  7. More shots may be added for personal preference
