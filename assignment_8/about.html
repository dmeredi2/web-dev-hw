My Inspiration to the World's Best Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Peanut butter and jelly sandwiches have always been one of my favorite foods. All throughout grade school my favorite lunchtime meal was a homemade peanut butter and jelly sandwich with the crust sliced off. In the summer on 2013, my FAVORITE aunt Pam came to visit by brother and I as she did each year for the summers because she was a school teacher. Pam has always been the best cook, and baker, and loved to try new recipes. When she arrived, she had a book full of new recipes to try, and when I flipped the front page over, there it was A GRILLED PEANUT BUTTER AND JELLY SANDWICH! I was so excited to try this recipe. Thursday night came along, which was my night to choose what was cooked of course, I choose this recipe. Little did I know it would become one of my favorite snacks in the future. This recipe is something that is super each to make for friends, and even family. Typically, everyone had these simple ingredients in their kitchen, but grilling the sandwich make it that much BETTER... and sadly fattening... worth it! If you would like to learn more about making these sandwiches, google has numerous recipes, and ideas. People get super creative and they and add all sorts of food items to the sandwich such as bananas and even BACON. This recipie holds a very special spot in my heart, and is something that I will pass on for generations to come, I hope you enjoy this tasty treat!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of jelly

Directions

  1. Heat up frying pan on low heat
  2. Place one tablespoon of butter in the frying pan and allow it to melt completely
  3. Spread 1/4 cup of peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Spread 1/4 cup of jelly on the remaining slice of bread
    5. Place two slices of bread together
  5. Once the pan is heated up, and the butter is heated completely place the sandwich in the frying pan
  6. Cover the pan and allow the sandwich to cook for two minutes
  7. Flip the sandwich over to ensure that both sides are cooked and browned evenly
  8. Once the sandwich is cooked to your preference place the sandwich on a plate and serve!
  9. More time may be added if a crisper sandwich is desired
