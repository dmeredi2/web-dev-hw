Grilled Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of peanut butter
  • 1/4 cup of jelly

Directions

  1. Heat up frying pan on low heat
  2. Place one tablespoon of butter in the frying pan and allow it to melt completely
  3. Spread 1/4 cup of peanut butter on one slice of bread
  4. Spread 1/4 cup of jelly on the remaining slice of bread
    5. Place two slices of bread together
  5. Once the pan is heated up, and the butter is heated completely place the sandwich in the frying pan
  6. Cover the pan and allow the sandwich to cook for two minutes
  7. Flip the sandwich over to ensure that both sides are cooked and browned evenly
  8. Once the sandwich is cooked to your preference place the sandwich on a plate and serve!
  9. More time may be added if a crisper sandwich is desired
